Top 10 World's Most Endangered Animals

10

Hawksbill Turtles

place Atlantic, the Indian, and the Pacific Ocean

Reason: Accidental capture in fishing gears...

9

Sumatran Orangutan

place Sumatra, Indonesia

Reason: Loss of forest habitat.

8

African Forest Elephant

place West and Central Africa

Reason: Poaching, habitat loss due to land-use for agriculture.

7

Black Rhinos

place Kenya, Namibia, South Africa, and Zimbabwe

Reason: Poaching for their horn.

6

Yangtze Finless Porpoise

place Yangtze River, China

Reason: Environmental degradation, overfishing, and water pollution.

5

Tapanuli Orangutan

place Sumatra, Indonesia

Reason: Loss of habitat due to agriculture, mining, and hydroelectric and geothermal development.

4

Mountain Gorillas

place Democratic Republic of Congo, Rwanda, and Uganda

Reason: Loss of habitat due to the expansion of human settlement.

3

Sunda Island Tiger

place Sumatra, Indonesia

Reason: Loss of habitat due to the expansion of human settlement and poaching.

2

Amur Leopard

place Russia and Northeastern China

Reason: Loss and fragmentation of habitat, prey scarcity, and transportation infrastructure such as roads.

1

Javan Rhinos

place Java, Indonesia

Reason: Hunting, loss of habitat, invading plant species that leave them with less food. Also, they are extremely vulnerable to extinction due to natural catastrophes, disease, poaching, and potential inbreeding.
